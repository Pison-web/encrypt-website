Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to Encrypts — an anonymous messaging platform built to keep your identity and data secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information and protect your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide a secure experience:

  • Account Info: first name, last name, email (for registration and verification)
  • Usage Data: inbox creation, link sharing, login activity
  • Anonymous Messages: messages received through public links are not tied to sender identity

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Manage your account and inboxes
  • Deliver and store messages securely
  • Send verification or password reset emails
  • Prevent abuse or spam

3. Data Storage and Security

Encrypts uses Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. All passwords are encrypted, and all data transmission occurs over SSL-secured connections.

4. Anonymous Messaging

Messages you receive are private and accessible only to you. We never view, track, or share your anonymous messages with any third party.

5. Account Deletion

Users can delete their account anytime. This permanently erases your profile, inboxes, and messages from our system.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Encrypts does not use cookies for ads or analytics. Local storage is used only to keep you logged in and improve your browsing experience.

7. Third-Party Services

Encrypts relies on trusted third-party tools:

  • Firebase – Authentication & Firestore database
  • GitHub Pages – Website hosting

These services comply with global privacy laws such as GDPR and CCPA.

8. Children’s Privacy

Encrypts is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data at any time from your profile page. For further assistance, contact us below.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, reach us at:
