About Encrypt

Encrypt is a simple, privacy-first tool for creating a private inbox that accepts anonymous messages from anyone with your public link.

What is Encrypt?

Encrypt allows you to create a unique inbox link and a public send link. Share the public send link with friends, followers, or colleagues. Messages sent through the public link arrive anonymously in your private inbox.

Why use Encrypt?
Privacy first

No sign-ups, no email addresses. Create a link and receive messages privately.

Fast & simple

Generate a link instantly and start receiving messages immediately.

Safe sharing

Post your public link anywhere—socials, bio, or chat groups.

Control

Open your private inbox to read, pin, or delete messages.

How it works
  1. Create your unique Encrypt link on the homepage.
  2. Share the public send link wherever you like.
  3. Open your inbox link to read and manage messages.
Our mission

We exist to make honest, anonymous communication simple and accessible—whether it’s feedback, confessions, or kind words. We believe privacy encourages openness.
